PNEUMONIA DETECTION

Importance of detecting Pneumonia At Early Stage

Pneumonia is an infection that causes inflammation in one or both of the lungs and may be caused by a virus, bacteria, fungi or other germs. So Detection at early stage can help a person to know whelther he/she is affected . If Affected the person need to be hospitalized as soon as possible.
